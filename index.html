Confidentiality & AI Policy

Your unpublished work is confidential. I do not share it, discuss it, or use it for any purpose other than your editing.

I do not use generative AI in my work. Your text is never uploaded to any AI platform. My feedback and edits come solely from my own assessment.

I encourage minimal use of AI in your text as it often flattens voice, and expert readers can detect its use.
